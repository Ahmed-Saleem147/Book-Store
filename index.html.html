A little about me

Hi — I'm glad you found this little corner of the internet.

I've been selling books from home for years, mostly at local markets and to friends. Going online felt like the natural next step. My stock is small and always changing — I only carry books I've personally read or can genuinely recommend.

If you ever want to ask about a title, need a suggestion, or just want to chat books — feel free to reach out. That's the best part of running a shop like this.

No newsletters. Just the occasional real update.
